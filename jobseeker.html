Temporary

Many clients use temporary talent, contractors with specific skillsets and disciplines to augment a project team, migrate to newer versions or programs, update systems, or launch new operating systems and software. For highly specialized talent, these assignments are ideal as they are usually for specific roles, and have defined start and end dates. Candidates filling these roles must be highly skilled, flexible and quick to adapt to the assignment and the company culture.

Temp-To-Perm

Often, clients with small IT staffs choose to grow their team methodically and chose to hire talent for a specific assignment with the prospect of offering the contractor a full-time position. This temp-to-perm arrangement allows for both the talent and the client to become acquainted with each other before committing to a long-term relationship. Candidates accepting potential temp-to-perm employment opportunities are often generalists or generalists-with-focus and thrive because of their with willingness to accept a full-time position with a company offering the right work-culture.

Permanent

Many of the placements for permanent employees are for generalists in small departments and for specialists in large organizations with multiple sub-specialty IT departments. Permanent placements become employees of our clients receiving all of the benefits of all other employees.
At NG Tech, we endeavor to make certain the talent we present fully understands the assignment, and the personalities of the candidates and the company are a good ‘fit”.
