Why NG Tech

NG Tech has filled thousands of public and private sector opportunities with talented people. Assignments range from contract work to permanent hires; resident and remote employees. When working with NG Tech every candidate is thoroughly vetted to ensure the talent requirements are met and the company culture is a good fit. Submit a resume today and learn how NG Tech can introduce new opportunities.

temp/contract

Many of our clients use temporary talent, contractors with specific skillsets and disciplines to augment a project team, migrate to newer versions or programs, update systems, or launch new operating systems and software. For the highly specialized talent, these assignments are ideal as the are generally for specific roles and have defined begin and end dates. Candidates filling these roles must be highly skilled, flexible and quick to adapt to the assignment and the company culture.

temp-to-perm

Often, clients with small IT staffs choose to grow their team methodically and chose to hire talent for a specific assignment with the prospect of offering the contractor a full-time position. This temp-to-perm arrangement allows for both the talent and the client to become acquainted with each other before committing to a long-term relationship. Candidates accepting potential temp-to-perm employment opportunities are often generalists or focused-generalists with an openness to accept a full-time position with a company offering the right work-culture.

perm

Many of the placements for permanent employees are for generalists in small staffs and for specialists in large organizations with multiple sub-specialty IT departments. Permanent placements hired employees of our clients receiving all of the benefits of all other employees. At NG Tech, we endeavor to make certain the talent we present fully understands the assignment and is a good ‘fit’ for the company culture.
