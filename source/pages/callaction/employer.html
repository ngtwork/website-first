talent

contractors

Contractors are often the best solution when IT teams are backlogged and a project needs immediate, sometimes specialized attention. At NG Tech, we have filled the gap when an employee has taken a sabbatical for family time, health needs, an extended vacation or any of a myriad of reasons. Contractor assignments have lasted several weeks, several months, and often have exceeded a year.

temp-to-perm

Rapid-growth companies often have an expanding and unforeseen need for IT services. A Temp-to-Perm placement allows for BOTH the candidate and the hiring manager to determine if the requirements and the company culture are a good fit. Once the IT professional is in place both parties can artfully assess the talent-needs, and candidate-capabilities. Often referred to a ‘test drive’ this can be the BEST solution for growing companies.

permanent hires

Permanent employees are the foundation of companies large and small. NG Tech through our large network of talent can help any company/agency find the BEST talent solution from the Help Desk to the Executive Suite. With 20 years of introducing companies and talent, our resources extend far beyond those of other staffing firms.

NG Tech's Global Presence

Generalists

Generalists serve with a broad range of engineering or IT knowledge - a 30,000-foot view of the industry landscape. These individuals often make excellent managers because they see the interconnectivity and the overlap between technical specialties.

Specialists

Highly trained in specific disciplines, specialists are hyper focused and very often, task-oriented. Large departments will often have banks of specialists dedicated to unique aspects of a company’s IT requirements.

Generalists With Focus

A true hybrid, we place technology and engineering talent who entered the workforce as generalists and have increased their value by intensifying their depth of specific disciplines. These individuals demonstrate some of the greatest promise with their willingness and ability to expand their knowledge. Their flexibility makes them ideal employees at small and large firms alike.
