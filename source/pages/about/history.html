Founded in 2000, Next Generation Technology has provided millions of man-hours of IT, Engineering and other professional talent to all levels of public sector (local, state and federal agencies) and large and small private companies.

While technology plays a sourcing-role for attracting talented candidates, key to our success at NG Tech is the personal interaction assuring the right fit between client and candidate. The appreciation of NG Tech’s human touch in the Information Technology staffing industry has been the reason clients have asked us to help search for other important professional staffing needs including engineers, at all levels.

NG Tech has sales executives in the US and offshore. Our corporate headquarters are in Schaumburg, Illinois (a Chicago suburb) and also home to the VMS and government liaisons. Additional sales staff work in Hyderabad (the capital of the Indian state of Telangana), the primary location of the recruitment team of the company.

Today, NG Tech has more than 50 recruiters supporting the sales efforts of more than 7 sales executives.
Next Generation Technology is a certified by the Women’s Business Enterprise National Council as a Women’s Business Enterprise which affirms NG tech is woman-owned, operated and controlled.
