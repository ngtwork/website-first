Next Generation Technology is a certified by the Women’s Business Enterprise National Council as a Women’s Business Enterprise which affirms NG tech is woman-owned, operated and controlled.

Founded in 2000, Next Generation Technology has served the public and private sector with millions of man-hours of IT, Engineering and other professional talent. NG Tech recognizes the value of technology as a source for finding talented candidates while believing personal interaction is required to determine the right fit between client-and-candidate culture. The recognition of NG Tech’s human touch in the information technology industry led clients to reach out to us to help find other important professional staffing needs including engineers, at all levels.

2000

Schaumburg

NG Tech has sales executives in the US and offshore. The corporate headquarters are in Schaumburg, Illinois (a Chicago suburb) and home to the VMS and government liaisons. Additional sales staff work in Hyderabad (the capital of the Indian state of Telangana) which is the primary seat of the recruitment team of the company.

Today, NG Tech has more than 50 recruiters supporting the sales efforts of more than 7 sales executives.
